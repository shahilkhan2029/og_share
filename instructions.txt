==========================
OG SHARE - INSTRUCTIONS

https://www.youtube.com/@OfGuru
==========================

1. Make sure Python is installed.
   Check with:
   python --version

2. Keep these two files together:
   - og_share.py
   - og_share.bat

3. Double-click og_share.bat to start the app.

4. Your browser will open automatically.
   - Scan the QR code on your phone to connect.
   - Or open the displayed URL (e.g., http://192.168.x.x:8000).

5. To share files:
   - Drag & drop files or click the upload area.
   - Files appear automatically in the interface.

6. To download files:
   - Click the file name or the Download button.

7. To stop the server:
   - Click "Stop Server" in the top-right corner.
   - Or close the terminal window.

8. All shared files are stored in the "shared" folder created automatically.

That's it. Enjoy simple file sharing!
